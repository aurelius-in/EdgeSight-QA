Edgesight QA installed in namespace: {{ .Release.Namespace }}

1) Verify pods:
   kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

2) Port-forward services:
   kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/capture 9001 &
   kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/preprocess 9002 &
   kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/inference 9003 &
   kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/results-adapter 9004 &

3) Health checks:
   curl -s http://localhost:9001/healthz
   curl -s http://localhost:9002/healthz
   curl -s http://localhost:9003/healthz
   curl -s http://localhost:9004/healthz

4) Start demo and watch events:
   curl -X POST http://localhost:9001/start
   curl -N http://localhost:9004/events
